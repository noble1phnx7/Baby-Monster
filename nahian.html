About Me


Nahian Sharif

I am a 4th year student at the University of Houston-Downtown. I am from Bangladesh, and I moved to USA in 2015. When I was in High school, I had no school friends because I didn't know how to speak English properly. Language barrier and cultural gap has caused me to face a lot of problems, but video games and online friends kept me motivated and calm during these tough times. Friends were not always there, but video games were. That's why I entered the University to learn programming so, I can make video games that can keep people away from stress and feel hopeful. During my time at the University, I realized that I can also solve many complex problems as a Software Engineer. That means, people can move forward without worrying about these issues that I already solved. It doesn't matter if I become a game developer, or Software Engineer as long as I am helping others to stay away from stress and dark times.
